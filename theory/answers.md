## RegEX

1. Single regex that matches either of these:

    antelope rocks out

    antelopes rock out

    Regex: `/antelopes? rocks? out/g`

2. Regex that matches either goat or moat but not boat:        Regex:`/(g|m)oat/g`

3. Regex that matches dates in YYYY-MM-DD format:

   Regex: `\d{4}-\d{2}-\d{2}`
